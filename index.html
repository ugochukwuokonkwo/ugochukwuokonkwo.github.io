  • Applied iterative prototyping and 3D printing to refine custom components and collector assemblies.
  • Conducted testing and parameter optimization involving flow rate, collector speed, and polymer delivery.
  • Implemented safety-focused design features including enclosure protection, grounding considerations, and high-voltage isolation.
